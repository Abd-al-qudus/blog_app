Admin Department: Provides administrative support to the agency's management. Manages legal and corporate matters. Handles pension and welfare matters, including social and health-related issues. Oversees the procurement and distribution of facilities and supplies. Bursary Department: Ensures efficient handling of government revenue. Manages the payment system in compliance with relevant laws and financial circulars. Handles budgeting and final accounts for government funds. Facilitates electronic payments for approved expenditures. Waste Management Department: Organizes and manages waste disposal, collection, and recycling facilities. Responsible for waste treatment and street cleaning operations. Aims to promote sustainable development and protect human health through effective waste management practices. Environmental Impact Assessment Department: Ensures that decision-makers consider the environmental impacts of projects. Provides essential information to key decision-makers to determine whether a project should be approved. Focuses on environmental impact assessment as an important tool for informed decision-making. Store Department: Manages and maintains records of items collected during encroachment activities. Responsible for inventory control and documentation of collected materials and equipment. Legal Department: Conducts regular epidemiological surveys and controls communicable diseases. Monitors and enforces environmental and public health laws. Plays a vital role in maintaining public health standards and ensuring legal compliance in environmental matters.
